Digital Expense Management

Optimize your employee and company expense administration and approvals from any location.

Optimize expenses processing, anywhere-anytime.

Managing employees’ expenses digitally in China is a powerful way to optimize and speed up business administration, simplify remote submissions and approvals, and minimize expense and approval errors. It is also a fundamental element for improving your internal control management.

Dezan Shira & Associates helps clients to manage their employee reimbursement and vendor payment procedures, using software that facilitates the digitization and application and approval of expenses via a mobile and desktop APP. Our solutions make staff reimbursement applications highly efficient, streamlining the monthly accounting processes, and provide greater transparency and real-time detailed expenses reporting.

We offer our digital Expense Management solutions as a value-added component to our accounting services customers, or to other customers as a stand-alone service solution that we can help them to integrate within their current accounting processes and technology environments.

How Companies can use it to Optimize their Expense workflows:

Integrated with China’s Pay Environment

The integrated data services not only verify ‘fapaio receipts’ validity, but add rich expense details, provided by some of the most frequently used suppliers in China for travel, office equipment and supplies, and banking and credits cards.

Key Benefits

Normal/Classic Expense Management: Digital Expense Management
Claims Process Slow and time-consuming manual processing of all steps:
  • Input of receipts and expense data - slow and prone to error.
  • Creating expense reports, - Submitting and discussing reports,
  • Gaining approvals, and manual processing.
 → Quick to execute steps, within a streamlined process:
  • Scan expenses via mobile APP (99% fapiao recognition), to save time and increase expense input accuracy.
  • Mobile App collects expenses for report submittals to be routed automatically for easy approval and processing.
Receipt Validation
  • Verifying genuine receipts is time consuming,
  • Redo’s due to improper receipts is a time-loss.
  • Gaining approvals, and manual processing.
 →
  • Scanned expenses via mobile APP already auto-validated with the tax bureau system at time of submission.
  • Can automatically identify previously submitted fapiaos.
Internal Control
  • Depends on manual verifications of many steps: receipt validity; expense allocation to category; matching the receipt to the claim;
  • Often hindered by knowledge gaps between departments about expenses, and across the languages of receipt, submitters and approvers.
 →
  • Expenses allocated to match your COA categories (flexible to configure).
  • Bilingual CHN-ENG APP allows expense, submission and approval steps be performed across languages, case by case.
Reports
  • Historical cost analyses are challenging to manually compile
  • Analysis of spend trends and details is difficult
  • - Graphical representations if any are manually “built”.
→
  • Historical expense reports available within the APP
  • Cross-period reporting and drill down detail
  • Graphical analysis to provide quicker trend insights
Data Capture
  • Expense transactions entered manually into ERP system
  • Limited amount of data enters system
  • Risk of human error is higher
→
  • Scan fapiaos with phone camera
  • Data synced with AP and T&E modules in ERP
  • Subsequent GL transactions populated from these modules
  • Far lower risk of manual entry error
Scalability As you grow to larger operational scales,
  • Processing workloads increases in step with scale.
  • Managing internal controls compounds in its increased importance and difficulty, without the right digital systems.
 → As you grow to larger operational scales,
  • Workloads decrease, compounding efficiency optimization
  • Data synced with AP and T&E modules in ERP
  • Managing internal controls remains inherently improved, while processes are adaptable to more stringent measures.

How to Get Started

Implementing Digital Expense Management into your company, is quick and painless. For small firms that have simple expense categories and approvals processes, only minor configuration is needed and can be up and running within one to three days.
