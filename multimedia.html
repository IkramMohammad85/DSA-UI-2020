China Regulatory Watch - 2019 Year in Review

Multimedia
Posted On December 2019

Presenter(s):

  • Holly McCleery

    Associate, Marketing Shanghai Office

Title: China Regulatory Watch - 2019 Year in Review
Date: December 20th, 2019

Welcome to Dezan Shira & Associates’ China Regulatory Watch podcast series. Every week we discuss the past week’s important news developments and highlight items we are keeping an eye on as they develop.

With 2020 just around the corner, we thought it would be interesting to look back at some of the biggest changes for foreign investors in China’s regulatory environment in 2019. This week, we discuss the Trade Deal, VAT and Intellectual Property reforms, a push for FTAs and more 2019 measures and laws.

Subscribe to us on iTunes or Stitcher and stay uptodate on China’s regulatory environment. Find us under the name, “Asiapedia: Discover Asia’s Business Environment”.
