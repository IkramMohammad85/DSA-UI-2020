Accounting Software

Accounting in today’s fast-moving digital economy, expects more than an excel spreadsheet, an email or the latest accounting App. Technology must work without burying you in unnecessary processes and save you time and money.

In China, our teams can either use your in-house China-compliant accounting system, or if you do not have one, we will provide you ours free of charge, as an included part of our basic services, and pass on the efficiency savings back to you as a value add.

Secure and integrated- We process clients’ transactions and reporting using secure, enterprise grade financial and reporting software. Our system is a secure Microsoft processing and file sharing environment, which we have tailored to meet the needs of small or medium sized customers, and integrate with important productivity, reporting and back office apps.

Better Data and Controls- Our accounting method provides a more controlled process with clear internal checkpoints, and more robust datasets which we leverage on your behalf throughout your accounting cycle. At each step, we can put the right person onto the job, to produce better and more streamlined outcomes without overcharging fees.

Analytics Dashboards- Each month, to compliment the monthly reports that you receive, and the communications with your Accounting Manager, you also receive access to online PowerBI reports. These reports can be used from any device to check your monthly finances, or search for certain expense items, with just a few clicks.

Summary

The accounting processing software that we use internally provides:

  • easy-use online monthly report dashboards;
  • seamless integration with local tax software (China golden tax system);

Depending on clients’ needs, besides the included basic reporting dashboards, our accounting module can be optionally configured to integrate with other services and feature-rich tools:

  • Customized chart of accounts and reporting
  • Integration with banking institutions for remote and online payment approvals and processing;
  • Integrated Digital Expense Management software (PO’s and reimbursements);
  • Advanced reporting tools: Robust cloud-based accounting reports in near real time; • Online HR administration;
  • ERP

We utilize the same processing engine for all our locations, so that our clients with multiple location can enjoy the same standard in reporting and enjoy the cost benefits of a consolidated service.
