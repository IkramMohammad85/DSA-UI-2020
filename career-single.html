Careers

Working at Dezan Shira & Associates is about growth. That means developing skills, making a difference, and achieving goals to help colleagues and clients reach new heights.

Marketing Assistant, Spanish Desk

Location: China, Shanghai
Function: Marketing
Role: Assistant (Intern)

Internship

Providing assistance on the Spanish Desk:

  • Conducting research on legal, tax and finance matters in China and in the wider Asia region.
  • Writing articles in Spanish and/or English related to foreign direct investment (FDI) in Asia region and outbound direct investment (ODI) from China to Spanish-speaking countries.
  • Producing other forms of marketing content as required.
  • Translating existing articles and existing marketing material from English into Spanish.
  • Assistance with the management of existing relationships with Spanish Desk partners and clients.
  • Assistance with the development of new relationships with potential Spanish Desk partners and potential Spanish Desk clients.
  • Applicants must meet the requirements to work as an intern pursuant to the country where they will be hosted.

Providing assistance on the Marketing team:

Assist with the marketing of Dezan Shira & Associates and Asia Briefing services and products

  • Coordinate, develop and maintain relationships with partners and improve media relations
  • Organize and coordinate marketing campaigns and activities, including but not limited to events, media sponsorships, multimedia and syndication
  • Tracking and analysis of marketing efforts and strategies
  • Improve publication distribution channels and database
  • Keep abreast of the latest China regulatory issues
  • Assisting internal HR and administrative projects

If you have any interests in this position, please kindly send your cover letter and resume to lorena.miera@dezshira.com

Dezan Shira & Associates is a pan-Asia, multi-disciplinary professional services firm, providing legal, tax and operational advisory to international corporate investors. Operational throughout China, ASEAN and India, our mission is to guide foreign companies through Asia’s complex regulatory environment and assist them with all aspects of establishing, maintaining and growing their business operations in the region. With more than 25 years of on-the-ground experience and a large team of lawyers, tax experts and auditors, in addition to researchers and business analysts, we are your partner for growth in Asia.
