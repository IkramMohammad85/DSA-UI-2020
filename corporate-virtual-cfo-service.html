Corporate Virtual CFO Service

Agile guidance and oversight for the financial and operational management of your Asia company.

How a Virtual CFO can help your Organisation

A Dezan Shira & Associates’ Virtual CFO is an on-the-ground resource who works closely with your team to help you achieve your financial objectives, on an interim or annual basis. Your ‘vCFO’ can provide a customized range of services, relating to the guidance and oversight of financial budgeting, tax & cash flow management and more (further explained below). Such resources are not only better able to connect your local and group financial organizations, they are also backed by our teams of CPAs, tax professionals, accountants, lawyers and other experts - all at a fraction of the cost of an internal CFO.

Virtual CFO Services

What a Virtual CFO could help your company with:

  • Act as a point of contact for Group CFO / Financial Controller
  • Recruitment & supervision of internal finance teams;
  • Budget development, tracking and analysis
  • Management of bookkeeping
  • Cashier Management
  • Cash flow forecasting
  • Coordination of Audit and Reporting
  • Oversight of financial statements and tax filing preparation and submissions;
  • Analysis & interpretation of financial results
  • Payroll processing
  • Internal control structuring
  • Tax planning and optimization
  • Transfer pricing advisory and coordination
  • Periodic virtual meetings with group CFO or Board
  • Review of selected operational aspects, eg: overall expenses; contract templates; etc.
  • Maintenance of bank & tax office relationships
  • Guidance and coordination of supplier contract review, dispute resolution, and negotiation.
  • Coordination of DSA internal resources and external resources (if applicable)
  • Support DSA-supported local expense reimbursement management app.

Key Benefits

A virtual CFO for your regional Asia business can be far more cost efficient than sending an executive from your own organization to lead your strategic financial operations. Yet, doing so also provides local expertise which can guide your firm through its phases – from early growth, through challenges and transformation, to restructuring and optimized profit strategies. This may of particular value during an interim or transition period, or as a stage in your company’s development.

A Virtual CFO (vCFO) also provides your organization with expertise more robust and dynamic than an internal hire. Backed by teams of multi-disciplinary service experts, you gain through the vCFO, ready access to experts in the areas of law human resources, payroll, technology and other disciplines.

With a Dezan Shira & Associates vCFO, capability to provide comprehensive support exceeds that of even larger accounting firms. Our ‘blended services operating model’ enables smooth intra-team collaboration on projects. This ensures that our clients receive fluid, practical cross-disciplinary corporate services, to meet their changing needs.

More concretely, this capability can provide rapid access to performing legal contract reviews, gaining advice on HR law, revising employment policies to adapt to recent regulatory changes, or presenting options for corporate restructuring, profit repatriation, auditing, improved IT infrastructure, and more.

Temporary or outsourced CFOs and CHRO’s can take more practical and unbiased views of potential impacts to your company, to provide guidance to, or complement your in-house teams. An external advisor or manager can be more objective when looking at which tasks to undertake in many situations. It can sometimes be particularly difficult for local internal teams to remain unbiased when monetary, contract and relationship factors conflict, or do not align with the profit and compliance strategies that are optimal for or were set out by your organization.
